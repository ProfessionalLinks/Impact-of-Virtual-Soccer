What is Virtual Soccer?

Virtual soccer, also known as esports soccer or digital football, refers to online video games or simulations that mimic the gameplay and dynamics of real-life football. Popular video games like FIFA, Pro Evolution Soccer (PES), and Football Manager allow players to control virtual teams, participate in tournaments, and compete in leagues. These digital platforms offer immersive experiences where players can virtually experience the thrill of the game, albeit in a digital environment. Over the years, virtual soccer has gained massive popularity, evolving into a competitive esports industry with professional players, global tournaments, and a massive online following.
